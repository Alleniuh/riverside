About us

RIVERSIDE COUNTY SHERIFF'S DEPARTMENT

Riverside County Sheriff's Department, created in 2018 by Random_Law is one of the biggest law enforcement groups on Roblox.

Sheriff Random_Law owned a group prior to this, called SDPD, or San Diego Police Department and it housed over 17,000+ employees. It was later shut down, hence the birth of Riverside County Sheriff's Department.

The Riverside County Sheriff's Department is an immersive and dedicated community that operates as the main law enforcement in the County. We are committed to upholding the law and ensuring the safety of our residents, we offer an engaging and realistic role-playing experience.

Our group provides a platform for members to assume various roles, from patrol officers to detectives and SWAT team members, fostering a sense of teamwork, communication, and community engagement. We pride ourselves on offering comprehensive training programs, exciting in-game events, and realistic roleplay for passionate law enforcement enthusiasts.

Join us in making Riverside County a safer place, one role-play at a time!
